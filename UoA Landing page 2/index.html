How to install Whatfix Extension for Chrome browser

Step 1
Click 'Add to Chrome'.
Step 2
Click 'Add extension'.
Step 3
The Extension has been installed.
Step 4
Go to Chrome settings, Extensions & click Manage Extensions.
Step 5
Voila! You are done.
